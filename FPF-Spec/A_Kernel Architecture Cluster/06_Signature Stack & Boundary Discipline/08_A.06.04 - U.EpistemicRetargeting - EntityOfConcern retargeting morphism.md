## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:1 - Problem frame

Many important operations on descriptions **change the EntityOfConcern** while preserving a structural or behavioural invariant:

* **Physical vs functional reinterpretation.**
  An episteme about a physical module (cabinet, rack, device) is reÔÇæinterpreted as an episteme about a functionÔÇæholon it realises. This is precisely what [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] `StructuralReinterpretation` loci express when a transformation-flow structure records this reinterpretation.

* **Signal vs spectrum.**
  A timeÔÇædomain signal description is reÔÇætargeted to a description of its frequencyÔÇædomain spectrum. The underlying invariant (typically energy or innerÔÇæproduct) is preserved, but the EntityOfConcern changes from `timeÔåÆvalue` trajectories to `frequencyÔåÆamplitude/phase` distributions.

* **Data vs model.**
  An episteme about raw observations (dataset) is turned into an episteme about a learned or estimated model, keeping an invariant such as likelihood, sufficient statistics, or predictive performance.

All of these are **EpÔåÆEp transforms** that:
* operate on Description epistemes, including Description epistemes admitted for specification use rather than mutating the EntityOfConcern itself,
* do **not** merely slice or re-express an episteme with the same EntityOfConcern (that would be EpistemicViewing, [[00_A.06.03 - U.EpistemicViewing - EntityOfConcern-preserving morphism|A.6.3]]),
* but **do change** the **EntityOfConcern/grounding bundle** (`EntityOfConcernSlot` and usually `GroundingHolonSlot`) under a formal bridge between kinds.

We need a single, reusable notion of **ÔÇ£epistemic retargetingÔÇØ** that captures these operations as:
* **effectÔÇæfree** at the level of Work/Mechanism (EFEM discipline),
* **EntityOfConcern retargeting** in a controlled way,
* **invariantÔÇæconservative** (no violation of the declared invariant between kinds),
* and **functorial** (retargetings compose cleanly and align with Bridges).

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:2 - Problem

Without a dedicated pattern for EpistemicRetargeting:
1. **Retargeting is silently confused with viewing.**
   Structural reinterpretations (e.g., componentÔåÆfunction, signalÔåÆspectrum, dataÔåÆmodel) can be mistakenly treated as ÔÇ£just another viewÔÇØ with the same EntityOfConcern, even though they change `entityOfConcernRef`. This hides the fact that the **EntityOfConcern** has changed and that a `KindBridge` and invariant are required.

2. **Invariants float untyped.**
   FourierÔÇæstyle moves, structural reinterpretations, and abstraction/refinement steps are often justified by ÔÇ£energy is preservedÔÇØ, ÔÇ£this component realises that functionÔÇØ, or ÔÇ£this model summarises those dataÔÇØ ÔÇö but these invariants are not connected to the episteme morphism class. Without a dedicated species:

   * invariants remain only in prose,
   * CLÔÇæpenalties and ReferencePlane crossings cannot be tracked systematically (Part F).

3. **CrossÔÇækind reasoning has no canonical morphism.**
   A general EFEM ([[06_A.06.02 - U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing - Effect-free morphisms of epistemes|A.6.2]]) can change `entityOfConcernRef` by setting `entityOfConcernChangeMode = retarget`, but:

   * nothing states what that means at the level of kinds (`Kind(entityOfConcernRef(X))` vs `Kind(entityOfConcernRef(Y))`),
   * nothing connects these moves to `KindBridge` and ReferencePlane policies.

4. **StructuralReinterpretation is adÔÇæhoc.**
   [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] positions `StructuralReinterpretation` as a transformation-flow locus, but its retargeting semantics are the generic ÔÇ£retargeting under a bridgeÔÇØ relation governed here, not a special graph-position ontology. Without a core pattern:

   * StructuralReinterpretation risks duplicating retargeting logic,
   * other discipline packs may reinvent their own adÔÇæhoc reÔÇætargetings.

5. **EntityOfConcern and Description-episteme boundary and specification-use discipline is left underspecified.**
   For Description epistemes and Description epistemes admitted for specification use (`...Description` and `...Spec`), retargeting **changes `EntityOfConcernRef` in `DescriptionContext = Ôƒ¿EntityOfConcernRef, BoundedContextRef, ViewpointRefÔƒ®`** ([[04_E.10.D2 - EntityOfConcern, Description Episteme, and Specification-Use Discipline|E.10.D2]]), but must say what happens to context and viewpoint. Without an explicit pattern, these decisions get scattered across different EÔÇæpatterns instead of being governed centrally.

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:3 - Forces

* **Changing the EntityOfConcern vs constructing something new.**
  Retargeting expresses **ÔÇ£describing a different but bridge-related entity through an explicit bridgeÔÇØ**, not arbitrary construction of a new EntityOfConcern claim/episteme. The invariant holds **across** the pair of entities, not inside a single episteme.

* **Invariants may be lossy but must be explicit.**
  A retargeting is often **lossy** (e.g. dataÔåÆmodel, signalÔåÆspectrum, structuralÔåÆfunctional view), but:

  * it must preserve an explicitly declared invariant (energy, behaviour, statistics),
  * any additional commitment must be modelled as a new or changed EntityOfConcern claim with its own Description epistemes, including Description epistemes admitted for specification use, not as a hidden side-effect.

* **Bridges and CLÔÇæpenalties.**
  Retargeting often crosses:
  * KindÔÇæplanes (different `Kind(U.Entity)`),
  * ReferencePlanes (different observability or abstraction regimes).
    Part F already has `KindBridge`, plane Bridges and CLÔÇæpenalties; EpistemicRetargeting must **reÔÇæuse** them instead of introducing its own notion of ÔÇ£linkÔÇØ.

* **Functors over `╬▒ : Ep ÔåÆ Ref`.**
  In the fibred view of epistemes ([[00_C.02 - Epistemic holon composition (KD-CAL)|C.2]] / [[06_A.06.02 - U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing - Effect-free morphisms of epistemes|A.6.2]]), `╬▒ : Ep ÔåÆ Ref` maps each episteme to its EntityOfConcern. EpistemicViewing preserves ╬▒ (`╬▒(v) = id`). Retargeting must:
  * change ╬▒ in a controlled way (`╬▒(r) = b : RÔéüÔåÆRÔéé` in `Ref`),
  * align with `KindBridge` and plane Bridges used for those base reference arrows.

* **Slot discipline and modularity.**
  [[01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] and [[13_A.06.05 - U.RelationSlotDiscipline - SlotKind - ValueKind - RefKind discipline for n-ary relations (with slot-operation lexicon)|A.6.5]] give epistemes a precise `SlotKind`/`ValueKind`/`RefKind` structure, including `EntityOfConcernSlot` and `GroundingHolonSlot`. Retargeting laws must be stated **at the slot level**, not on adÔÇæhoc ÔÇ£fieldsÔÇØ, so they can be reused across [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]], MVPK, and discipline packs.

### [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:4.1 - Informal definition

> **Definition (informal).**
> `U.EpistemicRetargeting` is the **EntityOfConcern retargeting species** of `U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing`.
> A `U.EpistemicRetargeting r : X->Y`:
>
> * takes an input episteme `X` and produces an output episteme `Y`,
> * **changes** the value filling `EntityOfConcernSlot` (`entityOfConcernRef(Y) != entityOfConcernRef(X)`),
> * relates the kinds of the source and receiving EntityOfConcern values via an explicit `KindBridge` in the appropriate ReferencePlane,
> * preserves a declared **invariant** across the pair of entities (e.g. energy, behaviour, sufficient statistics),
> * is **effect-free** at the level of Work/Mechanism (EFEM discipline),
> * and composes functorially with other retargetings and viewings.

In [[01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] terms, `U.EpistemicRetargeting` **re-indexes** an episteme along a base-level bridge: it moves the `EntityOfConcernSlot` (and often the `<EntityOfConcernSlot, GroundingHolonSlot>` bundle) along a `KindBridge`, while re-expressing `content : U.ClaimGraph` and `referenceScheme` so that the declared invariant continues to hold at the receiving EntityOfConcern.

**ERÔÇæ0 - Species & EntityOfConcernChangeMode.**

* Any morphism `r:XÔåÆY` declared as `U.EpistemicRetargeting` **MUST**:
  * be a species of `U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing` ([[06_A.06.02 - U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing - Effect-free morphisms of epistemes|A.6.2]]), and
  * declare `entityOfConcernChangeMode(r) = retarget`.
* Consequently:
 * the pair `<EntityOfConcernSlot, GroundingHolonSlot>` is the **retargeted EoC/grounding bundle** for the change (as in [[01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] ┬º7.3: EntityOfConcernÔÇæbundle retargeting),
 * `EntityOfConcernSlot` is **writeÔÇæenabled** (unlike Viewing) but only under the constraints below,
  * there exist entities `TÔéü, TÔéé : U.Entity` such that:
    * `entityOfConcernRef(X) = TÔéü`,
    * `entityOfConcernRef(Y) = TÔéé`,
    * `TÔéü Ôëá TÔéé` (as Ref/identity), and
    * `Kind(TÔéü)` and `Kind(TÔéé)` are related by a `KindBridge` in Part FÔÇÖs sense (with declared CL^k).

**ERÔÇæ1 - Typed domain/codomain & EntityOfConcernÔÇæbundle behaviour.**

For any `r:XÔåÆY` in `U.EpistemicRetargeting`:

1. `X` and `Y` are instances of `U.Episteme` species whose episteme kinds both realise at least the core [[01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] slots (`EntityOfConcernSlot`, `GroundingHolonSlot?`, `ClaimGraphSlot`, `ViewpointSlot?`, `ReferenceSchemeSlot`) and obey [[13_A.06.05 - U.RelationSlotDiscipline - SlotKind - ValueKind - RefKind discipline for n-ary relations (with slot-operation lexicon)|A.6.5]].

2. At the SlotKind level:

   * `EntityOfConcernSlot`:
     * **MUST change** (`entityOfConcernRef(Y) Ôëá entityOfConcernRef(X)`),
     * the ValueKinds for the slot in the domain and codomain kinds **MUST** be related via an `EntityOfConcernClass` pair that the `KindBridge` covers (e.g. `PhysicalModule` Ôåö `FunctionHolon`, `Signal` Ôåö `Spectrum`, `Dataset` Ôåö `StatisticalModel`).

   * `GroundingHolonSlot`, if present:
     * is either preserved by reference equality (`groundingHolonRef(Y) = groundingHolonRef(X)`), or
     * changed only along a declared holonÔÇæBridge in the same ReferencePlane (for example, moving from one runtime to another under a deployment bridge) with CL^plane penalties recorded in Part F.

   * `ViewpointSlot`, if present:
     * is either preserved, or
     * changed only within a declared `U.ViewpointBundle` ([[03_E.17 - Multi-View Publication Kit|E.17]].1/E.17.2), with the corresponding `CorrespondenceModel` explaining how the invariant is maintained under the new viewpoint.

1. For any episteme that is a `ÔÇªDescription`/`ÔÇªSpec` ([[04_E.10.D2 - EntityOfConcern, Description Episteme, and Specification-Use Discipline|E.10.D2]]), `subjectRef` decodes to `DescriptionContext = Ôƒ¿EntityOfConcernRef, BoundedContextRef, ViewpointRefÔƒ®`. Under EpistemicRetargeting:
   * `EntityOfConcernRef` **MUST** change from `TÔéü` to `TÔéé` as in ERÔÇæ0,
   * `BoundedContextRef` is:
     * either preserved, or
     * changed along an explicit ContextÔÇæBridge ([[03_E.10.D1 - Lexical Discipline for ÔÇ£ContextÔÇØ (D.CTX)|E.10.D1]], Part F),
   * `ViewpointRef` is treated as in (2) above (preserved or mapped within a bundle), and any resulting change in admissible claims is governed by ERÔÇæ2.

The pair `<EntityOfConcernSlot, GroundingHolonSlot>` is treated as a **retargeted EoC/grounding bundle**: many practical retargetings work at the level of this bundle rather than EntityOfConcern alone, especially where [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] `StructuralReinterpretation` is used.

**ERÔÇæ2 - Invariant-based conservativity (lossy but admissible).**

Let `X` and `Y = apply(r,X)` with:
* `entityOfConcernRef(X) = TÔéü`, `entityOfConcernRef(Y) = TÔéé`,

* `content_Y`, `referenceScheme_Y`,
* `groundingHolonRef_X`, `groundingHolonRef_Y`.

Then:
1. There MUST exist a KDÔÇæCAL/LOGÔÇæCAL expression of `Inv` such that:
   * all claims about `Inv` that can be derived by interpreting `content_Y` through `referenceScheme_Y` relative to `<TÔéé, groundingHolonRef_Y>`
     **are entailed by**
     claims about `Inv` derivable from `content_X` through `referenceScheme_X` relative to `<TÔéü, groundingHolonRef_X>`.

2. Retargeting, as an EFEM instance, **may**:
   * discard information not needed to maintain `Inv` (lossy summarisation),
   * change representation schemes (e.g. time vs frequency domain),
   * move to different abstraction planes or ReferencePlanes (with Bridges and CL penalties declared),
   but **MUST NOT** violate the declared invariant.

3. Any intended change that adds commitments about `Inv` beyond what is derivable from `X` **is not a valid EpistemicRetargeting**. It must be modelled as:
   * a change of EntityOfConcern claim (new Description episteme or Description episteme admitted for specification use under [[00_A.07 - Strict Distinction (Clarity Lattice)|A.7]] and [[04_E.10.D2 - EntityOfConcern, Description Episteme, and Specification-Use Discipline|E.10.D2]]), or
   * a chain of retargetings and EntityOfConcern claim updates explicitly recorded in KDÔÇæCAL/LOGÔÇæCAL.

**ERÔÇæ3 - Functoriality, ╬▒ÔÇæreindexing & SquareLaw witnesses.**

EpistemicRetargeting **inherits EFEM functoriality** and specialises it to the retargeting case:

1. At the `Ep` level:
   * `apply(id, X) = X` (no retargeting),
   * `apply(rÔéé Ôêÿ rÔéü, X) = apply(rÔéé, apply(rÔéü, X))` whenever domains/codomains match,
   * the composite `rÔééÔêÿrÔéü` has `entityOfConcernRef(X) = TÔéü` and `entityOfConcernRef(cod(rÔééÔêÿrÔéü)) = TÔéâ`, with a composed `KindBridge(TÔéü,TÔéâ)` whenever the Bridges of `rÔéü` and `rÔéé` compose.

2. At the `Ref` level, under `╬▒ : Ep ÔåÆ Ref`:
   * each retargeting `r` induces a base arrow `╬▒(r) : RÔéüÔåÆRÔéé` in `Ref`, compatible with the `KindBridge` used in ERÔÇæ0,
   * the square formed by:
     * `XÔåÆY` in `Ep` (retargeting),
     * `╬▒(X)ÔåÆ╬▒(Y)` in `Ref` (base retargeting),
     * any measurement or evaluation morphisms on either side,
       **MUST** commute **up to a declared SquareLawÔÇæretargeting witness** (Part F / [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]]), documenting that evaluating then retargeting vs retargeting then evaluating yields equivalent results (modulo CLÔÇæpenalties).

2. When retargetings use CorrespondenceModels between epistemes (e.g. aligning detailed hardware layouts with function networks), they MUST:
   * reference the CorrespondenceModel explicitly,
   * publish witness epistemes that certify commutativity of key squares, analogous to EVÔÇæ4 but now across **different EntityOfConcern values.**

**ERÔÇæ4 - Idempotency & determinism on fixed Bridge/invariant.**

For any `r:XÔåÆY` in `U.EpistemicRetargeting`, with fixed:
* `KindBridge(TÔéü,TÔéé)` and ReferencePlane policies,
* invariant `Inv`,
* configuration (ContextSlice, representation families, CorrespondenceModels),

the following MUST hold:

* **Idempotency.**
  Applying `r` twice does not further change the EntityOfConcern or invariantÔÇærelevant content:
  * `apply(r, apply(r, X))` is **isomorphic** (in the EFEM sense) to `apply(r, X)`,
  * `entityOfConcernRef` is already `TÔéé` after the first application,
  * `content` and `referenceScheme` differ at most by declared structural equivalence (e.g. normal forms at the receiving EntityOfConcern).

* **Determinism.**
  For fixed input `X` and fixed Bridge/invariant configuration, the result is uniquely determined modulo declared equivalence. Any source of nonÔÇædeterminism (randomness, time, external service state) MUST either:
  * be made explicit as part of `content`/`meta` of `X`, or
  * be moved to a `U.Mechanism` outside the retargeting morphism.

**ERÔÇæ5 - Applicability, EntityOfConcernClass pairs & CLÔÇædiscipline.**

Each species of `U.EpistemicRetargeting` MUST declare an **Applicability profile** ([[04_A.06.00 - U.Signature - Universal, law-governed declaration for a SubjectKind on a BaseType|A.6.0]]) that includes:

1. **EntityOfConcernClass pairs.**
   Admissible pairs of `EntityOfConcernClass`es (ValueKinds of `EntityOfConcernSlot` for domain and codomain), for example:
   * `(PhysicalModule, FunctionHolon)`,
   * `(Signal, Spectrum)`,
   * `(Dataset, StatisticalModel)`.

   For each such pair, the pattern MUST reference the appropriate `KindBridge` species in Part F.

2. **Grounding constraints.**
   Permitted classes of `groundingHolonRef` and ReferencePlanes, including whether:
   * grounding must stay within the same holon,
   * or may move along specific holon Bridges with CL^plane penalties.

3. **Viewpoint/context constraints.**
   Whether retargeting is allowed for all viewpoints or only for specific `U.ViewpointBundle`s (TEVB etc.), and any requirements on `BoundedContextRef`.

4. **CLÔÇædiscipline.**
   Minimum CL^k and CL^plane required for the Bridges used, aligning with [[00_F.09 - Alignment & Bridge across Contexts|F.9]] and the [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] `StructuralReinterpretation` rules.

Any attempt to apply a retargeting outside this Applicability profile is **illÔÇætyped**.

**ERÔÇæ6 - Compatibility with Viewing and Mechanisms.**

1. **Separation from Viewing.**

   * Any morphism that **does not change** `entityOfConcernRef` (and keeps `EntityOfConcernChangeMode = preserve`) belongs to [[00_A.06.03 - U.EpistemicViewing - EntityOfConcern-preserving morphism|A.6.3]] `U.EpistemicViewing`, not to `U.EpistemicRetargeting`.
   * Any morphism that **does** change `entityOfConcernRef` **MUST NOT** be declared as `U.EpistemicViewing`; it is either:
     * a `U.EpistemicRetargeting`, or
     * a more general pattern that composes several retargetings and EntityOfConcern claim changes.

   In any composite `VÔêÿr` or `rÔêÿV`, entityOfConcern changes are localised to retargeting steps; Viewing steps are always `entityOfConcernChangeMode = preserve`.

2. **Separation from Mechanisms.**

   * Retargeting MAY depend on outputs produced by `U.Mechanism` (e.g., computing a Fourier transform, fitting a model), but those are separate Work/Mechanism steps.
   * `U.EpistemicRetargeting` itself remains **effectÔÇæfree**: it rearranges epistemes, slots and ClaimGraphs, but does not perform measurements or actuation.

### [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:4.4 - Boundary with representation, explanation, transformation-flow structure, and neighboring claims

`U.EpistemicRetargeting` is triggered by changed EntityOfConcern, EntityOfConcern kind, ontology frame, admissible predicate set, or invariant-bearing receiving EntityOfConcern. It is not triggered by changed wording, changed representation scheme, changed explanation mode, or publication formatting alone.

Boundary rules:
- if the EntityOfConcern is preserved and the main change is representation scheme or reasoning medium, use [[03_A.06.03.RT - Representation-Scheme Transition- EntityOfConcern-Preserving Representation-Scheme Transition|A.6.3.RT]];
- if the EntityOfConcern is preserved and the main change is explanation mode, explanatory stance, or explanation-facing publication, use [[04_E.17.EFP - ExplanationFaithfulnessProfile - explanation-use discipline over existing MVPK faces|E.17.EFP]];
- if the source and receiving items are only bridge-only comparison, analogy, equivalence, or substitution relation, use [[00_F.09 - Alignment & Bridge across Contexts|F.9]] or [[01_F.09.01 - Bridge Stance Overlay|F.9.1]] instead of interpreting the bridge as identity;
- if the receiving item is useful only under narrower declared use with visible loss and source-bearing reopen, use [[01_A.06.03.CSC - Controlled Semantic Coarsening|A.6.3.CSC]];
- if decoded or latent output is interpretable but not tied to source claim, access relation, recoverability evidence, admissible-use value, and remaining reader action, keep it report-only, exploratory, source-bearing reopen, or in the named neighboring pattern;
- if a `StructuralReinterpretation`, `PathSliceId`, `CrossingRef`, or `DecisionLogRef` is present, use [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]], [[00_A.20 - U.Flow.ConstraintValidity - Eulerian|A.20]], or [[00_A.21 - GateProfilization- OperationalGate(profile) (GateFit core)|A.21]] for graph, path, constraint, and gate relations. Those references do not prove semantic continuity or retargeting admissibility by themselves;
- if changed problem formulation changes abductive prompt, candidate generation, rival-set formation, selected prime hypothesis, plausibility filtering, or abductive reopen, use [[00_B.05.02 - Abductive Loop|B.5.2]];
- if the receiving item is used as work, evidence, assurance, gate passage, temporal claim, dynamics law, or control relation, use [[00_A.15 - Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)|A.15]], [[00_A.10 - Evidence Graph Referring- Claim-Bound Evidence and Provenance Graph|A.10]], [[00_B.03 - Trust and Assurance Calculus (F-G-R with Congruence)|B.3]], [[00_A.21 - GateProfilization- OperationalGate(profile) (GateFit core)|A.21]], [[00_C.27 - Temporal Claim Adequacy- State Readings, Temporal Trends, and Intervention-Sensitive Temporal Change|C.27]], [[03_A.03.03 - U.Dynamics- State-Space and Transition-Law Episteme|A.3.3]], or the neighboring governing pattern.

`StructuralReinterpretation` in [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] receives retargeting semantics from this pattern. It is not an [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]]-local retargeting kind and not proof that the source and receiving items preserve the same `entityOfConcernRef`.

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:5 - Archetypal grounding (Tell-Show-Show)

**Tell.**
EpistemicRetargeting captures **ÔÇ£same invariant, different EntityOfConcernÔÇØ** moves:

* the source episteme describes ÔÇ£this cabinetÔÇØ, while the receiving episteme describes ÔÇ£the routing function it realisesÔÇØ;
* the source episteme describes ÔÇ£this signal over timeÔÇØ, while the receiving episteme describes ÔÇ£its spectrum over frequencyÔÇØ;
* the source episteme describes ÔÇ£this datasetÔÇØ, while the receiving episteme describes ÔÇ£a model class with parameters ╬© learned from itÔÇØ.

In each case, what remains stable is an **invariant** (behaviour, energy, likelihood), not the EntityOfConcern itself.

**Show 1 ÔÇö StructuralReinterpretation in [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]].**
* `X` describes a physical module holon `S_phys`.
* `Y` describes a function holon `S_func`.
* A `KindBridge(S_phys, S_func)` expresses ÔÇ£this module realises that functionÔÇØ.
* An [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] `StructuralReinterpretation` locus can be governed as an instance of `U.EpistemicRetargeting` when its invariant is the behaviour relation between `S_phys` and `S_func`.

**Show 2 ÔÇö SignalÔåöSpectrum.**
* `X` describes a timeÔÇædomain signal `s(t)`; `EntityOfConcernRef(X) = S_time`.
* `Y` describes its spectrum `S(¤ë)`; `EntityOfConcernRef(Y) = S_freq`.
* `KindBridge(S_time, S_freq)` encodes Fourier duality in the relevant ReferencePlane.
* The invariant is energy (or inner product), expressed as a KDÔÇæCAL statement; EpistemicRetargeting ensures that energyÔÇærelated claims in `Y` are entailed by `X`.

**Show 3 ÔÇö DataÔåÆModel.**
* `X` describes a dataset `D` (observations); `EntityOfConcernRef(X) = S_data`.
* `Y` describes a model `M` (e.g. a parametric family with learned parameters); `EntityOfConcernRef(Y) = S_model`.
* `KindBridge(S_data, S_model)` encodes the intended dataÔåÆmodel relation (e.g. MLE, Bayesian posterior).
* The invariant is likelihood or predictive performance; the retargeting laws ensure `Y` does not claim more about this invariant than is warranted by `X`.

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:6 - Consequences

* **Clear separation of Viewing vs Retargeting.**
  [[00_A.06.03 - U.EpistemicViewing - EntityOfConcern-preserving morphism|A.6.3]] and [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]] now jointly distinguish:
  * **views**: same `EntityOfConcernRef`, possible representation/viewpoint changes;
  * **retargetings**: different `EntityOfConcernRef` under `KindBridge` and invariants.

* **Canonical governing pattern for StructuralReinterpretation.**
  [[00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]] `StructuralReinterpretation` receives semantics from `U.EpistemicRetargeting`, not from an ad-hoc special graph-position kind. This reduces duplication and clarifies how CL penalties and Bridges are used.

* **Invariants become firstÔÇæclass.**
  Retargeting makes invariants explicit and typeÔÇæchecked: every such morphism must state what it preserves and how that is expressed in KDÔÇæCAL/LOGÔÇæCAL.

* **Safer crossÔÇæplane reasoning.**
  ReferencePlane crossings and kindÔÇælevel moves are handled via existing Bridges (Part F), with CL^plane/CL^k penalties and SquareLaw witnesses, instead of hidden in implementation details.

* **Better integration with EntityOfConcern and Description-episteme boundary and specification-use gate.**
  For `ÔÇªDescription`/`ÔÇªSpec` epistemes, retargeting is the only place where `EntityOfConcernRef` in `DescriptionContext` is allowed to change; all other EntityOfConcern and Description-episteme boundary and specification-use operations (Describe, specification-use refinement, Viewing) keep it fixed.

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:7 - Rationale & SoTAÔÇæechoing  *(informative)*
* **Fibrations and baseÔÇæchange (displayed categories, 2017+).**
  With epistemes forming a category `Ep` fibred over `Ref` via `╬▒ : Ep ÔåÆ Ref` ([[00_C.02 - Epistemic holon composition (KD-CAL)|C.2]] / [[06_A.06.02 - U.EffectFreeEpistemicMorphing - Effect-free morphisms of epistemes|A.6.2]]), EpistemicViewing corresponds to **vertical morphisms** (`╬▒(v) = id`), while EpistemicRetargeting corresponds to **reindexing along base reference arrows** (`╬▒(r) = b : RÔéüÔåÆRÔéé`). This lines up with baseÔÇæchange and transport along fibrations in category theory.

* **Structured cospans and reinterpretation.**
  Modern work on structured cospans and open systems uses cospans and their morphisms to move between different presentations of a system while preserving a notion of interface/behaviour. Retargeting plays a similar role: it moves from one entity kind to another while preserving a declared invariant.

* **FourierÔÇæstyle dualities.**
  In signal processing and physics, Fourier and related transforms are often treated as isometries between function spaces, preserving energy while changing the domain of discourse. `U.EpistemicRetargeting` abstracts this pattern: the invariant is codified in KDÔÇæCAL/LOGÔÇæCAL; the morphism explicitly changes the EntityOfConcern along a `KindBridge`.

* **Data/model duality in ML.**
  Contemporary ML practice cycles between data and models; invariants such as likelihood, risk, and calibration matter more than raw equality of ClaimGraphs. Retargeting gives a structured way to talk about dataÔåÆmodel (and, potentially, modelÔåÆdata) moves as episteme morphisms, rather than untyped ÔÇ£trainingÔÇØ steps.

* **Consistency management and abstraction.**
  In modelÔÇædriven and bidirectional transformation literature, abstraction and refinement transfers information between models with different subject domains. Treating these as retargetings with explicit Bridges and invariants makes their assumptions amenable to CL accounting and KDÔÇæCAL reasoning, instead of hiding them in tooling.

## [[08_A.06.04 - U.EpistemicRetargeting - EntityOfConcern retargeting morphism|A.6.4]]:9 - Mini-checklist (for use)

When you think you need "retargeting" in FPF, ask:

1. **Does `entityOfConcernRef` change?**
   If no, this is Viewing ([[00_A.06.03 - U.EpistemicViewing - EntityOfConcern-preserving morphism|A.6.3]]), not Retargeting.

2. **Is there a `KindBridge` between source and receiving entities?**
   If not, add or select the bridge in Part F, or revise the EntityOfConcern instead of treating the relation as retargeting.

3. **What invariant are you preserving?**
   Write it down in KD-CAL/LOG-CAL terms. If you cannot, retargeting is underspecified.

4. **How do `GroundingHolonRef`, context, and viewpoint behave?**
   State whether they stay the same, move along Bridges, or are out of scope.

5. **Can the operation be factored as Mechanism + pure retargeting?**
   If the step needs computation such as FFT or model fitting, separate the Mechanism from the EpistemicRetargeting.

6. **What remains admissible for the reader?**
   State the remaining reader action, and name source-bearing reopen or a neighboring pattern when the bridge, invariant, or source/bridge/invariant witness is insufficient for the intended use.
