## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:0 - Use This When

Use this pattern when a project needs to say how several admitted objects are considered as a whole, or when a whole-level claim depends on parts, membership, component structure, constructional grounding, or a selected aggregation rule.

Typical moments:

- a product, plant, dataset, paper, model family, organization, fleet, batch, or research program is discussed as a whole;
- a dashboard rolls part measurements into a whole-level characteristic;
- a team says that a method, role, graph, or algebra "decomposes" something and may be smuggling part-whole claims;
- a collection needs whole-level characteristics without becoming an acting collective system;
- an aggregation claim is being used for architecture, assurance, evidence, or MHT reasoning.

**First useful move.** Recover the claim kind before choosing notation: part-whole construction, membership, collection-as-whole grounding, role relation structure, method relation structure, work occurrence holarchy, selected architecture structure, or mathematical description.

**What goes wrong if missed.** Γ, graph, algebra, decomposition, factor, component, step, phase, and collection wording become one universal composition language. Roles and methods become parts; work occurrence evidence is inferred from method structure; a graph is mistaken for the structure; a collection becomes an acting whole by label.

**What this buys.** [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]] gives one doorway into part-whole construction while keeping its neighbors clean: [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/14_Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases/00_A.14 - Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases|A.14]] owns relation vocabulary, [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]] owns constructional grounding, [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/02_03_Trust and Assurance Calculus (F-G-R with Congruence)/03_B.03.05 - Working-Model Relations & Grounding (CT2R-LOG)|B.3.5]] owns Working-Model assurance grounding, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]] owns work-occurrence holarchy, and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] owns mathematical-lens use.

**Not this pattern when.**

- If the question is the local relation word, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/14_Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases/00_A.14 - Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases|A.14]].
- If the question is constructive part-whole grounding, use [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]].
- If the question is assurance grounding for a working model, use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/02_03_Trust and Assurance Calculus (F-G-R with Congruence)/03_B.03.05 - Working-Model Relations & Grounding (CT2R-LOG)|B.3.5]].
- If the question is role or method relation structure, use the role or method owner and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] when a mathematical lens is relied on for the current claim.
- If the question is performed-work occurrence parts, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]].
- If the question is whole reidentification or emergence-family wording, use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] or [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/01_B.02.P - Emergence and MHT Precision Restoration|B.2.P]].

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:1 - Problem Frame

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]] is not a universal algebra pattern. It is the holonic construction doorway for part-whole and collection-as-whole claims.

The older Γ material remains useful only after the ontology-side claim has been recovered. Γ, graph, algebra, tuple, matrix, embedding, and neural representation can express or check a selected structure; they do not decide by spelling that the current object is a holon, that the relation is parthood, or that a role, method, step, or work occurrence is a part of a holon.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:2 - Problem

Without [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:

1. **Mereology and notation collapse.** A graph or algebra is treated as the part-whole structure itself.
2. **Roles and methods become parts.** Role factors, method parameters, guards, steps, and compositions are read as holonic parts because the same word "decomposition" appears.
3. **Work occurrence is inferred from plan.** A method decomposition or schedule is treated as evidence that performed work had those parts.
4. **Collections become acting systems.** A set, list, batch, fleet, or community receives agency, responsibility, or capability without [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/01_Holon Ontic Foundation (U.Holon and Admitted Holon Kinds)/00_A.01 - Holon Ontic Foundation (U.Holon and Admitted Holon Kinds)|A.1]] and role-method-work admission.
5. **Emergence becomes rhetoric.** A whole-level gain is explained by "synergy" or "more than the sum" without checking existing-whole explanations or [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] whole reidentification.

```text

  boundedContextRef:

  constructionBasisRef?

  wholeLevelCharacteristicRefs?
  assuranceGroundingRef?
  neighboringNonPartRelationRefs?
```

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:4.3 - Work Occurrence Holarchy

Performed work is different from structural composition.

A work occurrence can have temporal parts, episode parts, operational parts, concurrent sub-runs, retries, resource roll-ups, and effect composition. That is a work-occurrence holarchy governed by [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]], not evidence that the method or role expression is a holonic part-whole structure.

Use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]] when the claim needs occurrence identity, temporal coverage, `Gamma_time`, `Gamma_work`, episode policy, overlap policy, resource aggregation, or performed-work evidence.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:4.4 - Mathematical And Representation Apparatus

Use Γ, graph, algebra, tuple, matrix, embedding, or neural representation only after the object under concern and selected relation are named.

Acceptable uses:

- a mathematical lens for a selected structure;
- a constructional expression for a part-whole claim already admitted by [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/14_Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases/00_A.14 - Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases|A.14]] and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]];
- a representation of dependency relations;
- a checking apparatus for invariants or conservative bounds.

Blocked uses:

- graph wording as parthood admission;
- algebraic factorization as role, method, or work parthood admission;
- source notation as a new U-kind;
- one fold rule as a universal replacement for the direct owner.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:4.5 - Existing-Whole Before MHT

Before declaring [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] whole reidentification, ask whether the whole-level gain can be explained inside the existing whole:

- better parts;
- corrected part relation;
- improved measurement;
- role or method relation repair;
- work occurrence evidence repair;
- functional or architecture selected-structure repair;
- source, publication, or representation correction.

Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] only when the whole itself must be reidentified.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:5.1 - Pump Skid

A pump skid can be a `U.System` holon whose parts include pumps, valves, controller, frame, and connectors.

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]] recovers the holon aggregation claim. [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/14_Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases/00_A.14 - Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases|A.14]] names component or portion relations. [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]] gives constructional grounding. [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]] carries whole-level characteristics such as maximum flow or pressure envelope. If a graph is drawn, it represents a selected dependency or structure; the graph is not the skid.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:5.2 - Evidence Corpus

A corpus can be a collection-as-whole under `MemberOf` and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]] set construction. Its whole-level characteristics may include coverage, source freshness, bias exposure, or evidential diversity.

The corpus does not become an acting system. A review board, script, or research team may be an acting system in role. The corpus may be an episteme or publication-side object under direct owners.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:5.3 - Method With Steps

A machining method has ordered steps and parameters. Those steps are method relation or method-description material. They do not prove that a performed machining run occurred, nor that the run had the same parts.

If the current claim is the method structure, use method owners and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] when algebra is selected. If the current claim is the actual run, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]].

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]]:8 - Consequences

- [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]] can still use useful aggregation apparatus without letting apparatus choose the ontology.

- Users must identify the claim kind before choosing a notation.
- Some Gamma-heavy examples require a direct owner before the notation is used: work occurrence evidence uses [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]], work-resource aggregation uses [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/06_B.01.06 - Work-Resource Aggregation|B.1.6]], mathematical-lens reliance uses [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]], and whole reidentification first uses [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/01_B.02.P - Emergence and MHT Precision Restoration|B.2.P]] and then [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] when the problem remains current.
