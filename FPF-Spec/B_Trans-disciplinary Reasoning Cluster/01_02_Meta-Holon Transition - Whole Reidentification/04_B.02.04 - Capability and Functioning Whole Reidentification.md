## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:0 - Use This When

Use this pattern when capability-envelope evidence, functioning-relation evidence, or transformation-flow-structure evidence creates or reveals a [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] whole-reidentification question.

The first useful question is:

```text
Does this capability or functioning evidence show that the existing whole
is no longer the right EntityOfConcern, or is this only a direct capability,
functioning, transformation, method, work, module, characteristic, or
architecture claim?
```

Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] only for the first case. Use direct owners for the second.

**What goes wrong if missed.** A genuine new whole is hidden under ordinary capability improvement; or every impressive capability, function, method chain, module allocation, or metric gain is overclaimed as emergence.

**What this buys.** The pattern keeps capability and functioning evidence available for [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] while preventing [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] from becoming a generic capability, function, method, work, module, or emergence pattern.

**Not this pattern when.**

- If the claim is ordinary capability, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/02_Role Taxonomy/02_A.02.02 - U.Capability - System Ability Envelope and Measures|A.2.2]] and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]].
- If the claim is function-like wording or functioning relation without whole reidentification, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/06_Signature Stack & Boundary Discipline/12_A.06.F - Function and Functional Precision Restoration (RPR-FUNCTION)|A.6.F]].
- If the claim is transformation or transformation-flow structure, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/04_U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions/00_A.03.04 - U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions|A.3.4]], [[E_The FPF Constitution and Authoring Guides/17_18_Transformation Flow Structure/00_E.18 - Transformation Flow Structure|E.18]], and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/18_30_Grounded Architecture and Selected-Structure Adequacy/07_C.30.TFS-REL - Architecture Transformation-Flow Structure Relation|C.30.TFS-REL]].
- If the claim is method, method relation, method description, work plan, or work occurrence, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/00_A.15 - Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)|A.15]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/01_A.03.01 - U.Method- Context-Defined Way of Doing|A.3.1]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/02_A.03.02 - U.MethodDescription- Description Episteme for a Way of Doing|A.3.2]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/02_A.15.02 - U.WorkPlan|A.15.2]], and [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]].
- If the claim is module allocation or bearer allocation, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/06_Signature Stack & Boundary Discipline/13_A.06.M - Module Relation Repair|A.6.M]] and architecture owners.
- If the claim is measurement, threshold, score, robustness, quality, or whole-level characteristic, use [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/19_CharacteristicSpace & Dynamics Hook (A.CHR-SPACE)/00_A.19 - CharacteristicSpace & Dynamics Hook (A.CHR-SPACE)|A.19]], and evidence owners.
- If the wording is ambiguous emergence, synergy, or title-mnemonic language, use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/01_B.02.P - Emergence and MHT Precision Restoration|B.2.P]] before selecting [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]].

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:1 - Problem Frame

A new capability is not automatically a new whole. A function-like relation is not automatically a part-whole relation. A transformation-flow structure is not automatically MHT.

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] is the narrow [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] specialization for cases where capability, functioning, or transformation-flow evidence makes the existing whole explanation fail and points to a reidentified holon. It is not a pattern for all capabilities or all functions.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:2 - Problem

Without this specialization:

1. **Capability becomes generic emergence.** A threshold crossing or new envelope is treated as a new whole without [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] checks.
2. **Function becomes ontology.** Function-like wording creates `U.Function` or a hidden peer kind.
3. **Method and work collapse.** The way of doing, description of doing, planned work, and performed work are compressed into one vague operational claim.
4. **Module allocation becomes functional truth.** A module label is treated as evidence for the required behavior or selected structure.
5. **Transformation-flow description replaces in-life structure.** A graph, diagram, or publication of a flow is treated as the flow structure or whole.
6. **Whole reidentification is missed.** A real result whole is left as "just a better capability".

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:4.3 - Existing-Whole Explanation

Use `ExistingWholeExplanationCheck@Context` before claiming whole reidentification.

Direct-owner explanations that often stop [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:

- better measurement or benchmark normalization;
- improved component capability;
- corrected function-like wording;
- a clearer method relation or method family selection;
- a new method description without performed capability evidence;
- better work coordination inside the same whole;
- module allocation repair;
- architecture-view or transformation-flow-structure repair;
- evidence or source-currentness improvement.

If one of these explanations is sufficient, do not use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]. Use the direct owner.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:4.4 - When [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] Returns To [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]

Return to [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] when the evidence shows that the current object must be reidentified as a result holon. Examples:

- a production cell now has a capability envelope, coordination relation, transformation-flow structure, and assurance claim that cannot be explained by individual machines;
- a service platform now has a functioning relation and external commitments that cannot be assigned to one service or module;
- a team, toolchain, and method family now operate as one result system with new capability and work evidence;
- an episteme or standard now has a capability for explanation, prediction, or specification use that requires result-episteme reidentification.

After the return, [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] owns the MHT record and result-kind admission. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] carries only the capability and functioning evidence slice.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:5.1 - Production Cell Capability

A milling machine, robot arm, fixture, inspection station, and scheduling rule can remain a collection of assets. A new production-cell whole becomes current only when capability and functioning evidence shows one bounded result whole: cell-level cycle time, tolerance, transformation-flow structure, coordination, and assurance cannot be explained by any single component or old aggregate.

Use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/06_Signature Stack & Boundary Discipline/12_A.06.F - Function and Functional Precision Restoration (RPR-FUNCTION)|A.6.F]] for function-like wording, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/04_U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions/00_A.03.04 - U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions|A.3.4]] for transformations, [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/18_30_Grounded Architecture and Selected-Structure Adequacy/07_C.30.TFS-REL - Architecture Transformation-Flow Structure Relation|C.30.TFS-REL]] for transformation-flow structure, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]] for performed work, [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]] for cycle-time and tolerance characteristics, and [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] only when the cell whole must be reidentified.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:5.2 - CI/CD Capability

A team may have methods for coding, testing, and releasing. That does not by itself create a new whole. Use method and work owners for the method relations and performed release work.

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] becomes current only if the capability evidence points to a result holon: a platform, team-system, or work occurrence whole with new delimitation, coordination, external commitments, evidence, and assurance. An automated delivery sequence label does not decide the ontology.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:5.3 - Theory Explains New Phenomena

A new theory may explain phenomena that the source portfolio did not explain. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] can carry the explanatory-capability evidence, but [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/03_B.02.03 - Meta-Holon Transition With Episteme Result|B.2.3]] owns the episteme-result MHT if the result is `U.Episteme`; [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/00_02_Epistemic holon composition (KD-CAL)/01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] owns the episteme slot relation; [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] owns mathematical-lens use when the lens is relied on for the current claim.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:8 - Consequences

Positive consequences:

- Capability and functioning evidence can bear on real whole reidentification without becoming a generic emergence owner.
- Direct owners remain visible, so local improvements are not overclaimed.
- Method, work, function, module, and architecture distinctions survive high-pressure capability language; each claim remains with its governing pattern.

Costs:

- Teams must do the direct-owner test before using [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]].
- Many impressive capability claims will stay outside MHT.
- [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] depends on [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] for the final whole-reidentification record.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]]:9 - Rationale

Capabilities and functioning relations are often where new wholes first become visible. But the evidence is mixed: it may belong to capability measurement, function-like wording, architecture structure, transformation flow, method relation, work occurrence, module allocation, or whole reidentification.

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] exists to keep that mixed evidence disciplined. It does not rename all of it as "meta-function". It asks whether the evidence defeats the existing whole explanation and, only then, returns to [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]].
