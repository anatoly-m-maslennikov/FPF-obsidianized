## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:0 - Use This When

Use this pattern when a configured whole can no longer be treated as the same whole for the current claim: its delimitation, objective, supervision, capability envelope, agency threshold, temporal consolidation, or bounded context has changed enough that the EntityOfConcern must be reidentified.

Typical moments:

- a set of coordinated parts becomes a regulated system with its own objective and externally visible commitments;
- a commissioning history crosses into operation and the assurance claim must restart for the operational whole;
- a theory, model family, or knowledge body becomes an admitted episteme whole rather than a loose catalogue;
- a capability envelope appears only after structure, functioning, method, work, and evidence align;
- an architecture residual cannot be explained inside the existing whole.

**First useful move.** Try `ExistingWholeExplanationCheck@Context` first. If the observed gain or shift can be explained inside the existing whole by better parts, corrected relations, improved measurement, method or work repair, richer phase coverage, or architecture-view repair, stay with the existing whole and its direct owners. Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] only when the whole itself must be reidentified.

**What goes wrong if missed.** Emergence becomes rhetoric, ordinary improvement is overclaimed as a new whole, or a genuinely new whole remains hidden under old part, evidence, assurance, architecture, or responsibility claims.

**What this buys.** [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] gives one accountable whole-reidentification move: recover the existing whole, trigger profile, result holon kind, identity claim, evidence, direct owner of changed content, and blocked overreads before any MHT claim is relied on.

**Not this pattern when.**

- If the claim is ordinary part-whole construction, use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/14_Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases/00_A.14 - Advanced Mereology- Components, Portions, Aspects & Phases|A.14]], and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/03_13_Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)/00_C.13 - Constructional Mereology (Compose-CAL)|C.13]].
- If the claim is a whole-level characteristic change, use [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]] and measurement or evaluation owners.
- If the claim is capability without whole reidentification, use capability and characteristic owners.
- If the claim is transformation or work, use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/04_U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions/00_A.03.04 - U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions|A.3.4]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/12_Acting-Side Externalization and Reflexive Split/00_A.12 - Acting-Side Externalization and Reflexive Split|A.12]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/00_A.15 - Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)|A.15]], and [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]].
- If the claim is only wording repair for emergence-family language, use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/01_B.02.P - Emergence and MHT Precision Restoration|B.2.P]] first.
- If the claim is graph, RG-like, MSPD, or other mathematical expression, use [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] unless whole reidentification is also current.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:1 - Problem Frame

A Meta-Holon Transition is not a new root ontology, not a generic emergence label, and not a mathematical graph result. It is a whole-reidentification claim about a holon in a bounded context.

The old whole remains a possible explanatory object. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] is selected only when the old whole is no longer the right EntityOfConcern for the current claim. The result can be admitted as a `U.System`, `U.Episteme`, `U.Work`, `U.BoundedContext`, `U.Discipline`, or another holon kind only when the direct governing pattern admits that kind and slot discipline.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:2 - Problem

Without [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:

1. **New whole is missed.** A coordinated closure or context reframe changes the object, but evidence and architecture still point to old parts.
2. **Ordinary improvement is overclaimed.** A better component, stronger measurement, or corrected method is called emergence.
3. **Record fields become ontology.** A result field, trigger mnemonic, profile, or checklist is treated as a U-kind or actor.
4. **Agency becomes binary.** A threshold crossing is read as "agent or not agent" instead of a characteristic-space threshold for a system in role.
5. **Mathematics replaces ontology.** A graph, RG-like flow, MSPD score, or benchmark jump is treated as MHT without recovering the holon claim.
6. **Transformation becomes containment.** A system changing another holon is treated as that holon's super-holon.

| Force | Tension |
| --- | --- |
| Parsimony vs real novelty | FPF should not mint new wholes for every improvement, but some closures really change the EntityOfConcern. |
| Continuity vs reidentification | History and phase continuity matter, but some transitions require a new result whole. |

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4 - Solution

Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] as a whole-reidentification pattern with three artifacts: a trigger profile, an existing-whole explanation check, and a holon reidentification record.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.1 - MHTTriggerProfile

`MHTTriggerProfile@Context` is a trigger and evidence profile for possible whole reidentification. It is not a U-kind and not MHT itself.

```text
MHTTriggerProfile@Context:
  existingWholeRef: U.Holon
  boundedContextRef:
  holonDelimitationChangeRef?
  objectiveOrEvaluationChangeRef?
  supervisionOrCoordinationChangeRef?
  capabilityOrClosureEvidenceRef?
  agencyThresholdRef?
  temporalConsolidationRef?
  contextReframeRef?
  evidenceRelationRefs:
  sourceUseRelationRefs?
  candidateResultHolonKindRef?
```

The profile asks whether enough has changed to make the old whole no longer the right EntityOfConcern. A single trigger is evidence for attention, not automatic admission.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.2 - ExistingWholeExplanationCheck

Before declaring MHT, run:

```text
ExistingWholeExplanationCheck@Context:
  observedGainOrShiftRef:
  existingWholeRef:
  explanationByBetterParts?
  explanationByCorrectedPartRelation?
  explanationByImprovedMeasurement?
  explanationByRaisedCongruenceOrSourceQuality?
  explanationByMethodOrWorkRepair?
  explanationByTemporalCoverageRepair?
  explanationByArchitectureViewRepair?
  explanationByCapabilityOrFunctioningRepair?
  remainingWholeReidentificationQuestion:
```

If an existing-whole explanation is sufficient, do not declare MHT. Use the direct owner for the repair.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.3 - HolonReidentificationRecord

Declare MHT only with a record that names the old whole, result whole, result kind, triggers, identity claim, and owner boundaries.

```text
HolonReidentificationRecord@Context:
  existingWholeRef: U.Holon
  boundedContextRef:
  selectedTriggerProfileRef: MHTTriggerProfile@Context
  existingWholeExplanationCheckRef: ExistingWholeExplanationCheck@Context
  mhtResultHolonRef:
  mhtResultSystemRef?
  mhtResultEpistemeRef?
  mhtResultWorkOccurrenceRef?
  mhtResultBoundedContextRef?
  mhtResultDisciplineRef?
  resultHolonKindAdmissionRef:
  identityContinuationOrReidentificationClaim:
  changedContentOwnerRefs:
  evidenceRelationRefs:
  sourceUseRelationRefs?
  mathLensUseRefs?
  blockedOverreads:
```

This record is not a U-kind and not an actor. It carries the reidentification claim and the direct owners of neighboring claims.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.4 - Result References

Use result references as fields, not as kinds:

- `mhtResultHolonRef` for the reidentified whole;
- `mhtResultSystemRef` only when the result is admitted as `U.System`;
- `mhtResultEpistemeRef` only when the result is admitted as `U.Episteme`;
- `mhtResultWorkOccurrenceRef` only when the result is admitted as `U.Work`;
- `mhtResultBoundedContextRef` only when a bounded context is itself the result whole under its direct owner;
- `mhtResultDisciplineRef` only when the result is a discipline holon under [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/08_20_Composition of U.Discipline (Discipline-CAL)/00_C.20 - Composition of U.Discipline (Discipline-CAL)|C.20]].

Do not use `post*` field names as live governed names. They hide the result kind and invite temporal shorthand.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.5 - Agency Threshold

Agency is not a binary status and not a root kind. Treat agency as a characteristic-space threshold for a system in bounded context.

Use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/13_The Agential Role & Agency Spectrum/00_A.13 - The Agential Role & Agency Spectrum|A.13]], [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/19_CharacteristicSpace & Dynamics Hook (A.CHR-SPACE)/00_A.19 - CharacteristicSpace & Dynamics Hook (A.CHR-SPACE)|A.19]], and [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]] for the characteristic-space and threshold claim. Levin-line TAME work can discipline the multi-characteristic framing when agency evidence is relied on for the current claim. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] uses agency threshold only as one possible trigger in `MHTTriggerProfile@Context`, and only when crossing the threshold changes closure, supervision, objective, or whole identity.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.6 - Acting-System Participation

When a source describes a system changing another holon, recover acting-system participation and transformation separately.

Use [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/12_Acting-Side Externalization and Reflexive Split/00_A.12 - Acting-Side Externalization and Reflexive Split|A.12]] for acting-side externalization, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/04_U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions/00_A.03.04 - U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions|A.3.4]] for bounded transformation, and [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/01_A.15.01 - U.Work|A.15.1]] for work occurrence. A system changing another holon does not become that holon's super-holon, and no `U.Transformer` kind is created.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:4.7 - Mathematical-Lens Separation

Graph, algebra, RG-like, MSPD, benchmark, scaling, and morphism language can bear on MHT recognition only as mathematical or analytical expression.

Use [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] when the mathematical lens is relied on for the current claim. Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] only after the holon identity claim is recovered and the existing-whole explanation check leaves a whole-reidentification question.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:5 - Archetypal Grounding (Worked Cases)

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:5.1 - Closed-Loop Regulated System

Parts: plant, sensor, controller, actuator.

Existing-whole repair may be enough if only a sensor improved or a controller parameter changed. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] becomes current when a closed supervisory structure and objective create a result system whose external commitments and capability envelope are no longer explainable as independent parts.

```text
MHTTriggerProfile@Control:
  existingWholeRef: plant-plus-devices configuration
  supervisionOrCoordinationChangeRef: closed feedback relation
  objectiveOrEvaluationChangeRef: maintain output y near reference r
  capabilityOrClosureEvidenceRef: capability envelope after closure

HolonReidentificationRecord@Control:
  mhtResultSystemRef: regulated control system
  resultHolonKindAdmissionRef: U.System admission under [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/01_Holon Ontic Foundation (U.Holon and Admitted Holon Kinds)/00_A.01 - Holon Ontic Foundation (U.Holon and Admitted Holon Kinds)|A.1]] and [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/02_B.01.02 - System Aggregation and Holon Delimitation|B.1.2]]
  changedContentOwnerRefs: control-structure view, transformation, capability, evidence
```

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:5.2 - Compendium Becomes Theory

A collection of results can remain a catalogue. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] becomes current only when the knowledge body is reidentified as an episteme whole with its own claim-bearing structure, explanatory objective, reference scheme, and evidence relations.

[[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/03_B.02.03 - Meta-Holon Transition With Episteme Result|B.2.3]] specializes this case when the MHT-result holon is admitted as `U.Episteme`. [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/00_02_Epistemic holon composition (KD-CAL)/01_C.02.01 - U.Episteme - Epistemes and their slot relation|C.2.1]] and the episteme family own episteme slot relation, publication, source-use, and claim-bearing structure.

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:5.3 - Capability Envelope Appears

Several systems, methods, and work occurrences align and a new capability envelope appears. Use direct capability, characteristic, function, transformation, method, work, evidence, and architecture owners first.

Use [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/04_B.02.04 - Capability and Functioning Whole Reidentification|B.2.4]] only when that capability or functioning evidence creates or reveals a whole-reidentification question under [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]].

### [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:5.4 - Lathe And Workpiece

A lathe transforms a workpiece. That is transformation and work, not MHT and not parthood. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] becomes current only if the manufacturing arrangement creates or reveals a new whole that must be reidentified, such as a production cell with closure, objective, coordination, and evidence that cannot be explained by the existing parts alone.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:8 - Consequences

Positive consequences:

- MHT becomes a precise whole-reidentification move rather than a synonym for improvement.
- System, episteme, work, bounded-context, and discipline result cases share one [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] spine while keeping their direct owners.
- Trigger language remains useful without becoming ontology.
- Mathematical and benchmark evidence can be used without replacing the holon claim.

Costs:

- Users must try existing-whole explanations before declaring MHT.
- MHT records require explicit result-kind admission and evidence.
- Some attractive emergence claims will return to ordinary characteristic, method, work, architecture, or measurement repair.

## [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]]:9 - Rationale

Holonic work needs a way to recognize when a whole has changed enough that the old EntityOfConcern no longer carries the current claim. [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/01_02_Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification/00_B.02 - Meta-Holon Transition - Whole Reidentification|B.2]] provides that move without collapsing all novelty into "emergence" and without inventing record-field U-kinds.

The pattern is intentionally conservative: it preserves ordinary direct-owner repairs first, then admits whole reidentification only when the existing whole no longer explains the observed shift. This protects [[B_Trans-disciplinary Reasoning Cluster/00_01_Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction/00_B.01 - Holon Aggregation and Part-Whole Construction|B.1]] part-whole construction, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/15_Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)/00_A.15 - Role-Method-Work Alignment (Contextual Enactment)|A.15]] work, [[A_Kernel Architecture Cluster/03_Transformer Constitution (Quartet)/04_U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions/00_A.03.04 - U.Transformation- Bounded Change Under Conditions|A.3.4]] transformation, [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/04_16_Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)/00_C.16 - Measurement & Metrics Characterization (MM-CHR)|C.16]] characteristics, [[C_Kernel Extension Specifications/17_29_Mathematical Lens Use/00_C.29 - Mathematical Lens Use|C.29]] math-lens use, and episteme and publication discipline from being swallowed by MHT.
